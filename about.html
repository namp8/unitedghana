OUR EXPERTISE

United Plastics and Packaging Industries Limited (UPPIL), a West African company led by Mr. Jagdish Jaisingh, was founded in 2003. Operating initially from a 2400 square foot warehouse, it began its operations with one single screw film extruder purchased from Argentina and three shopping bag cutting and sealing machines from South Korea.
Within four years, the company manufactured 250 million bags every month. Now, in 2016, the company is producing over 600 million shopping bags and over 50 million closures monthly. UPPIL has had consistent growth for the last 13 years and has a close connection to its key markets.
Mr Jaisingh is well known in the industry as being a pioneer for the production of premier, affordable and high-quality plastics. He has build loyal relationships with his clients who trust in the integrity of the UPPIL brand.
UPPIL sustains its key competitive advantages, which includes building strong relationships with its clients, providing high quality products at an affordable price and ensuring timely delivery, through its strong management team. Its organizational culture is based on a strong social conscience. It not only produces many 100% biodegradable products, but it also practices environmentally friendly initiatives, quality practices and ensures all staff are provided with a positive working environment and fair salaries and benefits.
